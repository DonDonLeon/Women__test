Every woman who starts her own business changes the around her and creates a new cluster

Many times I’ve seen women who want to start up their own business, but who are lacking in confidence and are scared to make a move? When we meet for breakfasts we share our business stories: from the initial idea to the break-even point and beyond. We discuss the failures and challenges all entrepreneurs face daily, and we prove that anything is possible if you work hard and are passionate about your project, no matter how hard it is to start, and we encourage others to follow our example.

The women who inspire project is my 3rd child, which was born in 2016, and i love it as much as i love my other 2 children.
